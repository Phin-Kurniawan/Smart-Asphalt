Development & Business Roadmap

Phase 1 · Years 0–5

Smart Lane Pioneer

Deploy 1–5 km motorcycle lanes on dense urban arterials. DWPT + DAS as a pilot project for city governments. Prove the technology, generate revenue through data subscriptions and energy contracts.

DWPTDASGov. contractsData subscription
Phase 2 · Years 5–10

Smart Corridor Expansion

Dynamic DWPT corridors, self-healing polymer asphalt, basic TEG energy harvesting. Target electric bus operators and last-mile logistics. Revenue through technology licensing and recurring energy fees.

Self-HealingTEGTech licensingRecurring fee
Phase 3 · Years 10–20

Smart Runway

High-precision surface with high-power DWPT for Ground-Effect pods. Serves intercity logistics hubs and freight companies. Revenue from runway leasing and per-km energy billing.

Urban Smart Grid

Photonic metamaterial asphalt, optional maglev integration. Becomes the national infrastructure standard and an active node in the urban electricity grid.
